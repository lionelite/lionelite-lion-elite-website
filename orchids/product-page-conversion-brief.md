# Lion Elite Wellness — Product Page Conversion Brief

## Objective
Rewrite every product page so visitors can understand the product in seconds without losing the research-use-only positioning of Lion Elite Wellness.

## Core rule
Use clear, human language. Avoid dense scientific wording, long paragraphs, exaggerated promises, medical advice, dosing guidance, or claims of guaranteed outcomes.

## Required page order

1. Product name and strength
2. One-sentence plain-language explanation
3. “What researchers are studying” with 3–5 short bullets
4. “How it is being studied” in one short paragraph
5. Product details: vial strength, format, storage information, testing/COA link, availability
6. Frequently asked questions
7. Research-use-only disclaimer
8. Lion Elite Beauty coaching cross-sell

## Writing style

- 7th–9th grade reading level
- Sentences under 20 words whenever possible
- Short paragraphs of 1–3 sentences
- Explain technical terms the first time they appear
- Use headings, bullets, icons, and generous spacing
- Sound premium, confident, educational, and easy to understand
- Never sound robotic or like a scientific abstract

## Standard section copy

### What is it?
[Product] is an investigational research compound studied for its interaction with [simple pathway or research category]. Researchers are exploring how it may influence [general research area].

### What researchers are studying
- [Research area 1]
- [Research area 2]
- [Research area 3]

### Why this research matters
Understanding these pathways may help researchers learn more about how biological systems communicate and respond under controlled laboratory conditions.

### Quality and transparency
Each product page should make the Certificate of Analysis easy to find. Display batch information, tested purity when verified, and storage details in a clean product-information panel.

### Coaching cross-sell
**Looking for personalized transformation support?**

Lion Elite Wellness provides research-focused products and education. Lion Elite Beauty provides coaching, accountability, nutrition guidance, training support, and a personalized transformation strategy.

Button: **Explore Lion Elite Beauty Coaching**

This section must not imply that research-use-only products are included in coaching or intended for human use.

### Research disclaimer
For laboratory research purposes only. Not for human consumption. This information is educational and is not medical advice. Products are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.

## Conversion improvements

- Place the simplest explanation above the fold.
- Make the COA visible without forcing the visitor to search.
- Add a sticky product summary on desktop.
- Add a clear mobile add-to-cart area where permitted.
- Add an FAQ accordion near the bottom.
- Add trust indicators for secure checkout, customer support, and transparent testing.
- Remove repeated disclaimers from the middle of the page; keep one prominent disclaimer near the purchase area and one complete disclaimer at the bottom.
- Avoid unsupported duration claims such as “5 mg lasts five weeks.” Duration depends on a protocol and should not appear on a research-use-only product page.

## Retatrutide example

### Retatrutide
**Investigational metabolic-signaling research compound**

Retatrutide is an investigational peptide studied for its interaction with three metabolic receptor pathways: GLP-1, GIP, and glucagon receptors.

### What researchers are studying
- Metabolic signaling
- Energy-balance pathways
- Glucose-related signaling mechanisms
- Interactions between multiple receptor systems

### How it works in research models
Retatrutide is called a “triple-receptor agonist” because researchers study how it activates three receptor pathways. Studying several pathways at once may help scientists better understand complex metabolic communication.

### Available research strengths
- 5 mg
- 10 mg
- 15 mg

Availability should be pulled from `public/data/current-inventory.json` and must never be hard-coded.

### FAQ

**Is Retatrutide FDA-approved?**
No. Retatrutide remains investigational and is not approved for human use.

**What does triple-receptor agonist mean?**
It means researchers are studying activity across three receptor pathways: GLP-1, GIP, and glucagon.

**Where can I find testing information?**
Place the current Certificate of Analysis directly on the product page when available.

**Do you provide personalized guidance?**
Lion Elite Beauty offers coaching, accountability, nutrition guidance, training support, and personalized transformation planning. Coaching is separate from Lion Elite Wellness research products.

## Brand separation

### Lion Elite Wellness
Research, laboratory education, product quality, COAs, scientific understanding, and research-use-only content.

### Lion Elite Beauty
Human coaching, accountability, nutrition, training, transformation, and personalized support.

The website should connect the brands without blending their legal or marketing purposes.
